
Welcoming Freshmen
The second year students at Stamford University are arranging a welcome party to their first year students. Each student while entering the party hall should say his/her name aloud. After a few seconds, a welcome message will pop up on a screen along with their name. Write a program to get the name from the user and display it along with the welcome message.

Sample Input

Enter the name

Brian rav

Sample output

Welcome Brian rav! Achieve with all your might.
